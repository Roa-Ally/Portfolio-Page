Hello, I'm Ally

I’m Ayleen but I go by Ally, a computer science student at FIU who’s been building things for a while now. I actually started programming before stepping into leadership, creating a few projects on my own and figuring things out through a lot of trial and error. Later on, I became Technical Projects Chair for SWE, where I led workshops and helped others build their own projects, which pushed me to grow both technically and as a leader.


Since then, I’ve worked across game development, front end, and interactive experiences, including time at Universal Creative on both the VACE and IXP teams where I built simulations and tools alongside the team. I’m strongest in Unity and C#, but I also work with React and web development, so I can move between building full experiences and designing clean, usable interfaces.


I like creating things people can actually use and enjoy. Whether it’s a game, a tool, or a website, I care about how it feels, not just how it works.
