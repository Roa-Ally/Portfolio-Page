Commander Coco's Stellar Domination

Overview

Commander Coco’s Stellar Domination is a 2D endless arcade shooter where players take control of Commander Coco, a seemingly cute but secretly ruthless bunny thrown into a chaotic battle for survival in space. The game combines fast paced shooting mechanics with a playful yet dark narrative twist, as Coco fights against increasingly difficult waves of enemies, including his evolutionary rival, the kitsune.


Players use simple controls for movement and shooting, making the game easy to pick up while still offering a challenging experience as difficulty ramps up over time. The endless gameplay loop encourages players to survive as long as possible, improving their reflexes and strategy with each run.


With its vibrant visuals, quirky concept, and engaging action, the game blends humor and intensity to create a unique arcade experience. It was developed during ShellHacks 2024 in just 36 hours, where it went on to win Best First-Time Hacker, highlighting both its creativity and execution under pressure.

Tools

  • Unity 2D
  • Git & GitHub
  • Aseprite

Process

Built within a tight 36 hour hackathon timeframe, development was highly fast paced and focused on rapid prototyping. The team prioritized getting a playable core loop running as quickly as possible, starting with basic movement and shooting. Once that foundation was in place, enemies and wave mechanics were added to introduce challenge and progression.


Due to the time constraint, decisions were made quickly, with a focus on functionality over perfection. Features were implemented in parallel, with team members working simultaneously on gameplay, visuals, and design. Continuous quick testing helped catch issues early and keep everything working together.


The final result reflects a high energy, collaborative development sprint, where creativity, adaptability, and teamwork were key to delivering a complete and award winning game in a short amount of time.
