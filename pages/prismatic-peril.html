Prismatic Peril

Overview

Prismatic Peril is a top down action defense game where players take on the role of the last Prism Guardian, tasked with protecting powerful crystals from waves of corrupted enemies. Set in a vibrant yet threatened world, the goal is to defend multiple crystal points, eliminate enemies, and restore light to areas consumed by darkness.


The gameplay combines movement, combat, and strategic positioning, with abilities like teleportation and special attacks adding depth to encounters. As enemies spawn around the map, players must balance offense and defense while managing multiple objectives at once. Through fast paced action and escalating difficulty, the game challenges players to survive, purify all crystals, and bring balance back to the world.

Tools

Unity 2D

Aseprite

Process

The game was developed through an iterative process, starting with core mechanics like player movement, combat, and crystal defense. From there, systems such as enemy spawning, abilities, and interactions were added and refined through playtesting. Visual assets and animations were created alongside development in Aseprite and integrated into Unity to enhance feedback and game feel. The team continuously tested and adjusted gameplay balance, difficulty, and pacing to create a more engaging and polished experience.
